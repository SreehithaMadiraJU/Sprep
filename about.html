📘 About Sprep

Sprep is an AI-powered study app designed to help students learn smarter, track their progress, and prepare effectively for exams — all in one place.

This app was born during AIHACKDAYS 2025, a hackathon conducted by Swecha Telangana. What started as a daring idea by three determined students became a complete solution for students who struggle with disorganized prep and lack of proper guidance.

🚀 Features at a Glance

  • 📚 Subject Pages: Organized learning spaces for Physics, Chemistry, and Maths.
  • 🤖 AI Chatbot: Instant, distraction-free academic assistance.
  • 📝 Mock Tests: Timed exams to simulate real pressure and sharpen accuracy.
  • 📄 Notes: Simplified, exam-focused content for quick revisions.
  • ❓ PYQs & Quizzes: Practice previous year questions and take fast quizzes to strengthen weak spots.
  • 🚦 Mistake Tracker: Review and reflect on your common errors.
  • 📈 Progress Analytics: Visual progress reports to measure how far you’ve come.
  • 🔥 Daily Streak System: Build consistent habits and stay accountable with every login.
  • 👤 Personalized Dashboard: Tailored experience based on your journey.
  • 🔐 Privacy-First: Only name and email collected. Your learning stays yours.
